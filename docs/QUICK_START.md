##  How to Use the New Features

### **Step 1: Paste or Type Your Content**
- Job posting, email, policy, etc.
- Any length, any context

### **Step 2: Click "Verify Text"**
Wait for analysis (AI is checking 7 types of bias)

### **Step 3: Read Your Score**
- Score tells you overall inclusivity level
- Risk level shows urgency
- Severity breakdown shows what to fix first

### **Step 4: Review Issues**
Look at each issue:
- Could be HIGH (discriminatory), MEDIUM (discouraging), or LOW (accessibility)
- Biased category is shown with color
- Explanation explains the impact

### **Step 5: Apply Suggestions**
- **Single fix**: Click "Apply Suggestion" on one issue
- **Fix all**: Click "Apply All" button
- Text updates instantly

### **Step 6: Re-verify**
After applying fixes, click "Verify Text" again:
- Score improves as biases are removed
- Watch the number of issues decrease
- Aim for 80+ inclusive score

##  Focus Areas by Content Type

### **Job Postings** 
Look for:
- Gender-biased language (he/she, rockstar, aggressive)
- Age discrimination (recent graduate, digital native)
- Over-qualification barriers (must have ALL skills)
- Physical ability requirements without accommodations

### **Emails** 
Look for:
- Blame language ("You failed...")
- Passive-aggressive tone ("Obviously", "Clearly")
- Inappropriate greetings ("Hey guys")
- Dismissive language

### **Policy Documents** 
Look for:
- Gender-specific terms (Chairman, stewardess)
- Binary gender assumptions
- Non-inclusive leave policies
- Missing accessibility clauses

### **Slack/Chat** 
Look for:
- Casual sexism
- Microaggressions
- Exclusionary humor
- Stereotypes

##  Common Questions

**Q: Can I ignore LOW severity issues?**  
A: They're optional, but fixing them improves readability.

**Q: How do I know what to suggest?**  
A: The app suggests the best alternative! Just click "Apply Suggestion".

**Q: Can I apply suggestions one at a time?**  
A: Yes! Each card has its own "Apply Suggestion" button.

**Q: What if I disagree with the suggestion?**  
A: You can edit after applying, or just ignore it.

**Q: Does this tool replace human review?**  
A: No - it's a guide. Always have humans review final content.
